About HomeSpark Automotive

HomeSpark Automotive is a one-man mobile car cleaning service built from the ground up with passion, precision, and pride. What started with cleaning my own car — and feeling proud to drive it around after — turned into something more. That result, that feeling, is exactly what I want every customer to experience.

Every detail matters, and every car gets the attention it deserves — whether it’s a quick exterior clean or a full deep interior refresh.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple: To deliver high-quality mobile car cleaning that’s professional, flexible, and leaves every customer 100% satisfied.

Whether it’s a one-off clean or a regular detail, we aim to give every vehicle the care it deserves — without dealership prices or long waits.

What Makes Us Different

  • ✅ Mobile — we come to you, saving you time
  • ✅ Fair pricing — no hidden charges or up-sells
  • ✅ Happy Guarantee — if you're not happy, we’ll make it right
  • ✅ Personal touch — you deal directly with the person cleaning your car

We don’t cut corners. We clean them.

Where We Work

We proudly serve Yorkshire and surrounding areas. From driveways to worksites, we bring our tools and products to you. At the moment, we use the customer’s water and power supply — but soon, we’ll be operating fully from our mobile cleaning van, allowing us to wash vehicles in any location.

Our Guarantee

Your satisfaction is our top priority. If you’re not happy with the results, we’ll come back and fix it — that’s our Happy Guarantee Policy.
